Introduction

The world we live in has become so addicted to materialism. Forgetting nature, the present society is habituated with mechanical life and hence unhealthiness has spread in various forms and is affecting a person's body, mind, spirit and social wellbeing. this is leading to the complete absence of peace, pleasure and happyness. in order to completely eradicate this, a 'Brahmastra', a holistic health approach, was started by name SPIRITUAL TABLETS. During the past 12 years, a lot of research has been done and now, this SPIRITUAL TABLETS RESEARCH FOUNDATION which was started with an aim to serve all is a pride for the whole world.

In the last 6 years, the group of spiritual counsellors and spiritual volunteers under the guidance of Dr Gopala Krishna has developed 350 spiritual tablets in the form of text, audio and audio visuals. These Spiritual Concepts will change our internal belief system and help us to transform ourselves in order to open to the Universe for its guidance in all circumstances. We conducted various training workshops which lead to the establishment of spiritual health care centres throughout India and abroad and trained 8,000 cases by teaching meditation, spiritual counselling, spiritual guidance and spiritual tablets.
